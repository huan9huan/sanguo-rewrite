## Style Guide

- Guan Yu's silence preserved through action, not reported speech
- Oath reads solemn but compressed — no archaic "verily" register
- Merchants' gift handled in swift beats, no ceremony
- Ending hook: "Five hundred men with no name had just walked through the gates"
