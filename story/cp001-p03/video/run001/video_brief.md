## MVP Goal

Make one 15-second vertical motion-comic short for foreign general viewers.

This is not a full retelling of the passage. It is a searchable short that explains why the Peach Garden Oath matters and gives the viewer one emotional reason to continue.

## Core Hook

Three unknown men did not begin with an army.

They began with a private oath.

## Viewer Promise

In 15 seconds, a viewer who does not know *Romance of the Three Kingdoms* should understand:

- Liu Bei, Guan Yu, and Zhang Fei become sworn brothers.
- The oath is not just friendship; it is a war promise.
- Their brotherhood quickly turns into a real fighting force.
- This is the emotional starting point of the Three Kingdoms story.

## Target Platforms

- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok
- Instagram Reels

Primary language: English

Visual style: black-and-white Chinese comic / motion comic

## Content Angle

Use a history-narrator voice, not a hype voice.

The tone should feel like:

- clear
- dramatic
- slightly mythic
- easy for a non-Chinese viewer to follow

Avoid:

- long cultural explanation
- naming too many places
- explaining every rank or dynasty term
- treating the oath as a trivia fact

## 15-Second Structure

| Time | Function | Content |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0.0-2.5s | Searchable hook | "Before the Three Kingdoms, three unknown men made one oath." |
| 2.5-6.0s | Character setup | Guan Yu arrives; Liu Bei and Zhang Fei recognize a man built for chaos. |
| 6.0-10.5s | Emotional center | In the peach garden, they swear brotherhood before heaven and earth. |
| 10.5-13.5s | Consequence | Horses, iron, weapons, and volunteers turn the oath into a force. |
| 13.5-15.0s | Hook forward | The three men walk into Youzhou and into history. |

## Required Frames

Use these current comic frames as the content basis:

- `f1`: Guan Yu arrives
- `f3`: Zhang Fei proposes the brotherhood
- `f4`: the oath in the peach garden
- `f6`: weapons and the force taking shape
- `f7`: entering Youzhou

Optional if timing allows:

- `f2`: the three men recognizing a shared road

Skip for MVP if too crowded:

- `f5`: horse merchants, unless needed to explain how they gained resources

## Dialogue Policy

Use mostly narrator.

Keep one short quoted line from the oath as dialogue-style emphasis:

"We swear to die together."

This line should feel solemn, not theatrical.

## Success Criteria

- The first 3 seconds make the viewer understand the topic.
- The oath feels like a story event, not a dictionary explanation.
- The video does not require prior knowledge of Liu Bei, Guan Yu, Zhang Fei, or Chinese history.
- The final second points forward to the wider Three Kingdoms story.
