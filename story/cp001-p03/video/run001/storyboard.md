# CP001-P03 Motion Comic Storyboard

Role: Motion Comic Director

Source brief: `video_brief.md`

Source script: `script_en.md`

Frame source: `story/cp001-p03/current/comic.png`

Output format: 9:16 vertical motion comic, 15 seconds

## Direction

This first MVP should feel like a narrated historical memory, not a fake full animation.

Use slow, deliberate motion:

- push in when the narrator names the oath
- hold still for the oath line
- move faster only when the oath becomes an army

Do not over-animate the comic. The style should preserve the black-and-white page feeling.

## Visual Thesis

The video moves from one man entering a tavern to three men entering history.

That means the motion arc is:

1. discovery
2. recognition
3. oath
4. mobilization
5. history opening

## Shot List

### Shot 1: The Search Hook

Time: 0.0-2.6s

Frame: `frames/f1.png`

Script:

"Before the Three Kingdoms, three unknown men made one oath."

Motion:

- Start on a full-width view of Guan Yu arriving.
- Slow push in toward the tavern figures.
- Keep the movement calm and centered.

Subtitle:

Bottom center. Keep clear of the main faces if possible.

Purpose:

Give the viewer the searchable term "Three Kingdoms" and immediately attach it to three unknown men.

### Shot 2: Recognition

Time: 2.6-5.8s

Frame: `frames/f2.png`

Script:

"Guan Yu came to enlist. Liu Bei and Zhang Fei saw the same road ahead."

Motion:

- Use a slight left-to-right pan across the three men.
- End with a tighter crop on their shared table or faces.

Subtitle:

Bottom center, two-line max.

Purpose:

Show that this is not random friendship. They recognize the same purpose.

### Shot 3: The Proposal

Time: 5.8-7.2s

Frame: `frames/f3.png`

Script:

"In a peach garden,"

Motion:

- Quick but soft cut from tavern to peach garden.
- Start wider, then push toward Zhang Fei and the other two.

Subtitle:

Bottom center.

Purpose:

Move the story out of the tavern and into the oath location without spending too much time.

### Shot 4: The Oath

Time: 7.2-10.8s

Frame: `frames/f4.png`

Script:

"they swore brotherhood before heaven and earth."

"We swear to die together."

Motion:

- Slow vertical push in on the kneeling figures.
- Hold nearly still during the quoted oath line.
- Add no comic bounce or shake here.

Subtitle:

- Narrator line at bottom.
- Oath line can appear centered slightly above bottom, in quotation marks.

Purpose:

This is the emotional center. The oath must feel solemn and readable.

### Shot 5: The Oath Becomes Practical

Time: 10.8-13.4s

Frame: `frames/f6.png`

Script:

"With horses, iron, and five hundred volunteers, the oath became an army."

Motion:

- Slight push in on weapons and armed brothers.
- If there is room, pan from Liu Bei to Guan Yu to Zhang Fei.
- This shot can be a little more energetic than the oath shot.

Subtitle:

Bottom center.

Purpose:

Translate the oath into action. The viewer should understand that brotherhood becomes military force.

### Shot 6: Into History

Time: 13.4-15.0s

Frame: `frames/f7.png`

Script:

"And the three men walked into history."

Motion:

- Start tighter on the group entering Youzhou.
- Very slight pull back or upward drift.
- End on a still frame for the last 0.3s.

Subtitle:

Bottom center.

Purpose:

End open, not closed. The story is beginning.

## Unused Frames

`frames/f5.png` is not used in this MVP. The merchants and resources are compressed into the narrator line over `f6`.

`frames/f3.png` is used briefly because the proposal matters, but the oath itself should dominate.

## Notes for Video Operator

- Target canvas: 1080x1920.
- Keep all subtitles inside a conservative safe area.
- Use white subtitles with thin black outline or black translucent backing, depending on readability over the frame.
- Use no synthetic mouth movement in MVP.
- Use no added color in MVP, except optional very subtle warm tint if a future style decision allows it.
- Add only light ambience if any: low room tone, wind in peach garden, distant crowd/army bed.
