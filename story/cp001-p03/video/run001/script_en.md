# CP001-P03 Short Video Script EN

Role: Short Video Editor

Target length: 15 seconds

Format: history narrator + one oath line

## Version 001

### Narrator

Before the Three Kingdoms, three unknown men made one oath.

Guan Yu came to enlist. Liu Bei and Zhang Fei saw the same road ahead.

In a peach garden, they swore brotherhood before heaven and earth.

### Oath Line

We swear to die together.

### Narrator

With horses, iron, and five hundred volunteers, the oath became an army.

And the three men walked into history.

## Subtitle Text

Before the Three Kingdoms, three unknown men made one oath.

Guan Yu came to enlist.

Liu Bei and Zhang Fei saw the same road ahead.

In a peach garden, they swore brotherhood before heaven and earth.

"We swear to die together."

With horses, iron, and five hundred volunteers, the oath became an army.

And the three men walked into history.

## Editorial Notes

- Keep the narrator calm and clear.
- Do not over-explain "heaven and earth"; the image and tone can carry it.
- "Three Kingdoms" is the searchable term.
- "Peach garden" is the story image.
- "The oath became an army" is the main interpretive line.
