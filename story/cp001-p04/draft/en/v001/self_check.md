## Style Guide

- Two battles clearly differentiated: first = raw power, second = tactical intelligence
- Liu Bei's strategic thinking shown through action (tracing lines on map)
- Fast combat rhythm — short sentences, no lingering
- Ending hook: "Their blades were not yet cold. The fighting was not yet done."
