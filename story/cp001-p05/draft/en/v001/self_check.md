## Style Guide

- Cao Cao's introduction = cinematic quick-cut, not biography
- Lu Zhi's scene = restrained dignity, no melodrama
- Dong Zhuo's insult delivered through action (wave of hand, walking away) not description
- Liu Bei's restraint vs Zhang Fei's fury = clean contrast
- Ending hook: "The blade was already at the tent flap." — mirror of Chinese ending
