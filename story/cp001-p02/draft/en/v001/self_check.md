## Style Guide

- Reads as original English, no translation tone
- Cultural terms implicit (zhang, chi left in as measurement — clear from context)
- Names in pinyin, contextualized on first mention
- Active voice throughout
- Ending hook preserved: "the shadow at the door moved first"
