Title: Before the Three Kingdoms: The Notice That Started Everything

Description:
A cinematic two-host story podcast from *Romance of the Three Kingdoms*.

Before Liu Bei, Guan Yu, and Zhang Fei enter the story, the Han empire is already cracking. Strange omens appear in the palace. The court stops listening. A healer named Zhang Jiao gathers followers, and the Yellow Turban Rebellion begins.

Then one recruitment notice appears on a city gate in Youzhou.

No one knows yet who it will bring.

This is Episode 1 of our podcast-driven motion comic adaptation for new listeners.
