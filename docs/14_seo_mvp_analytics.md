# SEO MVP Analytics

Use this note for the first ~20 natural users.

## Funnel events

- `landing_view`
- `landing_cta_click`
- `book_open`
- `chapter_open`
- `passage_open`
- `read_start`
- `comic_view`
- `next_passage_click`

## Retention signals

- `reading_30s`
- `reading_90s`
- `passage_scroll_50`
- `passage_scroll_90`

## Feedback and follow-up

- `language_switch`
- `passage_feedback_submit`
- `follow_subscribe_submit`
- `future_book_interest_submit`

## What to review

After the first ~20 natural users, check:

1. How many landing visitors click into reading.
2. How many passage readers reach `reading_90s` or `passage_scroll_90`.
3. How many readers click `next_passage_click`.
4. How often readers open `comic_view`.
5. Whether English readers fall off earlier than Chinese readers.
